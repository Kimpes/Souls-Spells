The spells of

Dark Souls

You would be hard pressed to find a game as influential to the modern landscape of video games as Dark Souls. Released in 2011, this game established many mechanics which have become staples since. Being an RPG to its core, it includes an intricately designed magic system in which spells are sorted into three categories, those being sorceries, miracles and pyromancies. In one playthrough, a player might only interact with a third of the game's magic. In this graph, I wanted to give credit to the graphic design of all spells, and compare their look to see if the three magic types create distinct groups when analysed visually. I also wanted to give the user the power to influence the graph and find their own interpretations, in a fashion not too dissimilar to how Dark Souls itself presents its story; open ended, and in the hands of the players.
